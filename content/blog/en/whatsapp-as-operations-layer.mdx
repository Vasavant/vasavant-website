Most mid-market companies already run on WhatsApp: trip coordination, delivery updates, incident photos, quick approvals. The issue is not another tool. It is that **critical information stays trapped in threads** disconnected from spreadsheets and ERP.

## From scattered messages to operational queries

An operations layer on WhatsApp enables:

- Asking for trip or asset status without opening five systems
- Logging events (delay, failure, delivery) with structured context
- Giving supervisors visibility without manual chat exports

This is not a marketing bot. It is **operational intelligence** on data you already produce, with governance and traceability.

## What must exist before the agent

A conversational agent is only as useful as the system underneath: identified sources, clear business rules, and role-based access. That is why we prioritize structure, automation, and dashboards before chat-based queries.
